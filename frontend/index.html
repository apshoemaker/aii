Real-time 3D Artemis II trajectory viewer with AI mission assistant.

Track NASA's Artemis II crewed lunar flyby mission as it happens — with live telemetry, JPL Horizons ephemeris data, Deep Space Network status, and an AI agent that can answer questions about the mission.

Data Sources

  • JPL Horizons — predicted trajectory vectors
  • NASA GCS — live spacecraft telemetry
  • NASA DSN — antenna tracking status
  • CelesTrak — TDRS satellite positions

Three.js, FastAPI, LangGraph, OpenRouter
